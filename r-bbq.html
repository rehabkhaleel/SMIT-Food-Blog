Grilled kofte (izgara köfte)

Grilled Lamb Kofta is one of the most popular street food in Turkey made with minced lamb and aromatic spices. It is extremely easy to make at home.

Ingredients

  • 1 large onion, coarsely grated
  • 50g flat-leaf parsley, finely chopped
  • 400g lamb mince
  • 400g beef mince
  • 3 tbsp Turkish sweet red pepper paste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • ½ tsp pul biber
  • 2 tsp dried mint
  • 1½ tsp freshly ground black peppercorns
  • 1½ tsp sea salt flakes
  • flatbreads, salad, hummus and chips, to serve

Instructions

  1. Put the grated onion in a sieve and squeeze out the juice before putting in a large bowl. Add all the other ingredients and, with clean hands, mix well. Divide and shape the mixture into 12 patties and lay them on a baking sheet.
  2. Put the baking sheet in the fridge and chill the patties for 1 hour, removing them from the fridge 30 minutes before you’re ready to cook them. To barbecue, make sure the coals are hot but mostly white and the grill is also very hot. Cook the patties for 6-8 minutes on each side, turning carefully for even cooking. Try not to turn them over until they have charred and sealed on the underside to prevent them from sticking to the grill.
  3. Alternatively, preheat the grill to medium-high. Line a baking sheet with foil and lay a grill rack on top. Evenly space the patties on the rack and cook for 6-8 minutes on each side, turning a couple of times. Serve in flatbreads with salad, hummus and chips.

Grilled Corn on the Cob

A traditional grilled corn recipe is served with butter and salt. It goes perfect next to grilled classic hamburgers, steak, grilled chicken or BBQ chicken. The pure taste of summer!

Ingredients

  • Corn
  • Flavored Butter for Corn
  • Seasoning

Instructions

  1. Peel back the husk, but leave it attached. Thoroughly clean the corn silk.
  2. Replace the husks and soak the whole cobs in clean water.
  3. Place the cobs on the grill, and turn every few minutes for about 15 minutes total.
  4. Add seasonings to melted butter and spread it on corn.
