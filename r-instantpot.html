Instant Pot Chicken Tortilla Soup

This is a delicious and easy-to-make soup that is perfect for a cold winter day. The chicken is cooked in a flavorful broth with tomatoes, corn, and black beans. It is topped with tortilla strips, cheese, and sour cream for a hearty and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 (15 ounce) can corn, drained
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • Tortilla strips, for serving
  • Shredded cheese, for serving
  • Sour cream, for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in the Instant Pot on the saut√© setting. Add the chicken and cook until browned on all sides.
  2. Add the onion, garlic, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper to the Instant Pot. Cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly.
  3. Add the diced tomatoes, black beans, corn, chicken broth, and cilantro to the Instant Pot. Close the lid and seal the valve.
  4. Cook on high pressure for 5 minutes, followed by a natural pressure release for 10 minutes.
  5. Discard the pressure release valve and carefully open the lid of the Instant Pot.
  6. Shred the chicken with two forks.
  7. Stir the soup and adjust the seasonings to taste.
  8. Serve the soup hot, topped with tortilla strips, cheese, and sour cream.

Instant Pot Lentil Soup

This is a hearty and healthy soup that is perfect for a cold winter day. The lentils are cooked in a flavorful broth with vegetables, such as carrots, celery, and onions. It is a great meal that is both filling and nutritious.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup lentils
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried rosemary

Instructions

  1. Rinse the lentils in a colander until the water runs clear.
  2. Add the lentils, vegetable broth, onion, carrots, celery, salt, pepper, bay leaf, thyme, and rosemary to the Instant Pot. Close the lid and seal the valve.
  3. Cook on high pressure for 8 minutes, followed by a natural pressure release for 10 minutes.
  4. Discard the pressure release valve and carefully open the lid of the Instant Pot.
  5. Stir the soup and adjust the seasonings to taste.
  6. Serve the lentil soup hot.
